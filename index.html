What is Rent to Own? Understanding the Basics

Rent to own (also called rent-to-own, lease-to-own, or lease-option) is an alternative path to homeownership that allows you to rent a property with the option or obligation to purchase it at a predetermined price after a specified period.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much do I need upfront for rent-to-own in Edmonton?

A: Typical upfront costs include an option fee of 3-5% of the purchase price ($12,000-$20,000 on a $400,000 home), first month's rent, and security deposit. Total: $15,000-$25,000.

Q: What credit score do I need?

A: Most Edmonton rent-to-own programs accept credit scores as low as 550-600.
